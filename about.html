Using "Likely" service is very easy, and it consists of only 4 steps, which we will explain carefully:

  • Step 2 : After pressing "Submit" button, you will be automatically transferred to the payment page and you can choose your favorite payment method there. [Note: The amount is fixed (1$) and there are only two ways to pay.]


  • Step 3 : If you choose the first payment method and after pressing "Pay Now!" button, you will be directed to a secure private "Skrill" page, where you will choose the payment options.


  • Step 4 : After we make sure that we have received the required amount and at a rate of 1 to 3 hours, you will start receiving our services automatically. [Note: In the case of any problem, you can contact us here.]
